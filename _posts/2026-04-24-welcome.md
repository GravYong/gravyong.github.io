This is the first post on a small blog I'm keeping alongside my personal site.

The plan is to write here occasionally — usually when I have something that's too long for a paragraph in a paper, too informal for a review, or too tangential to belong in the methods. The kinds of things I expect to end up here:

- **Behind a paper** — the part of a result that didn't make the manuscript: the numerical trick, the dead end, the plot I couldn't fit.
- **Notes on papers I'm reading** — short summaries when someone else's work is either so good or so puzzling that I want to write down what I took from it.
- **Methods and tooling** — if I build something reusable (a Mathematica notebook, a Python helper, a waveform-processing recipe), a post here is a better place to describe it than a buried README.
- **Occasional longer thoughts** on neutron-star physics, dense matter, gravitational waves, or the working life of a postdoc — when I actually have a thought worth writing down.

The tone will be informal. The pace will be irregular. If you came here from a paper or talk looking for the real output, the [publications]({{ site.url }}/publication/), [talks]({{ site.url }}/talks/), and [interactive companions]({{ site.url }}/research/#interactive-companions) pages are what you want.

More soon.
